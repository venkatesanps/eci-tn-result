Power Alternation

TN has alternated between DMK and AIADMK every 1–2 terms since 1977. No party has won three consecutive terms.

⊘

FPTP Seat-Vote Disconnect

In 2016, DMK+INC won more votes (43.5%) than AIADMK (40.8%) yet won 38 fewer seats — stark first-past-the-post effect.

↑

DMK Vote Share Growth

DMK+ vote share rose from 36.7% (2011) to 45.4% (2021), a gain of 8.7 percentage points over three cycles.

2026 Outlook

Pre-election analysis · Projections · Not official results

Key Factors

  • DMK incumbency: CM M.K. Stalin seeks re-election on development credentials.
  • AIADMK fragmentation: EPS-OPS split weakens the main opposition.
  • BJP/NDA: Aims to break into Dravidian politics. Won only 4 seats in 2021.
  • Anti-incumbency: Dravidian pattern favours rotation every 1–2 terms.
  • 50-yr milestone: 2026 marks 50 years of DMK-AIADMK dominance of TN politics.
